What Would Pepe Do

On our journey to a happy life, we channel the wisdom of Pepe to guide our choices. It's a path of contemplation, laughter, and community decisions. Just like Pepe, we find ourselves at crossroads of the absurd and the profound. As a united front, we vote on what Pepe would do, tackling life's dilemmas with a touch of humor and a lot of heart. Today's conundrum: pick a side. So, what would Pepe do?
